Student Mentoring

IIT-NIT Placement Support

IIT-NIT students from any program are welcome to join this Mentoring Program.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Sitting in the campus placement for the year 2025-26.
  • Seriously aiming to get an industry job.
  • Belonging to the Constitutionally marginalised Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.
  • Desire to give back to society.

"This is a free and voluntary service. Those receiving mentoring are expected to help others when they can in future."

How to Join:

1 Check if you meet all conditions listed above.
2 Submit the Google Form below.

Alumni Registration

Join as a Mentor

We invite alumni from IITs, IIMs, and NITs to join us in our mission to support students and uplift the community.

Why Join?

  • Give back to the community by mentoring deserving students.
  • Network with fellow professionals with a shared vision.
  • Participate in advocacy for representation and social justice.

Our Core Values

"We are non-political and work on trust, respect, integrity, and community service spirit. The Group respects everyone in the world."

If you agree with the Group's mission and values, you are warmly welcome to join us.
