The Mentoring Bharat Initiative

Launched in 2023, the Mentoring Bharat Initiative provides mentoring and peer support to IIT students from marginalised backgrounds, with a focus on academic guidance, career development, and community support.

About Us

Building Bridges for Social Justice

We are a professional networking and mentoring platform for IIT–IIM–NIT alumni from the Constitutionally recognised Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.

Inspired by principles of social justice and inclusion, our aim is to support alumni in their academic, professional, and personal growth, and to strengthen community networks through mentorship and mutual support. We are non-political and operate on values of trust, respect, integrity, and community service.

  • Promoting diversity and inclusion in higher education
  • Supporting academic and professional development through mentoring
  • Connecting a global network of 1,100+ active alumni members
Our Approach

Holistic Intervention Areas

We provide comprehensive support systems designed to address the unique challenges faced by students and alumni.

Emotional Support

Offering a safe space for conversation, encouragement, and peer guidance for students facing challenges, isolation, and discrimination.

Wellness Network

Access to a referral network of independent psychiatrists and mental health professionals. Any consultations are arranged directly between individuals and professionals.

Academic Growth

Supporting students in their academic journey through peer tutoring, resource sharing, and study planning with experienced mentors.

Career Guidance

Helping students navigate complex career landscapes, internship opportunities, and professional development paths.

Professional Networking

Creating a social capital pathway for alumni to advance their professional careers through referrals and networking.
