About Us

Building Bridges for Social Justice

We are a professional networking platform for IIT-IIM-NIT alumni belonging to the Constitutionally marginalized Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes.

Inspired by social justice philosophy, our aim is to support fellow alumni's academic, professional, and social progress as a means to pay back and uplift the community as a whole. We are non-political and work on trust, respect, integrity, and the spirit of community service.

  • Advocacy for diversity in higher education
  • Support for academic and professional progress
  • Over 1,000 active alumni members globally
Our Approach

Holistic Intervention Areas

We provide comprehensive support systems designed to address the unique challenges faced by students and alumni.

Emotional Support

Providing mental health and emotional guidance to students facing challenges, isolation, and discrimination on campus.

Academic Growth

Supporting students in their academic journey through peer tutoring, resource sharing, and study planning with experienced mentors.

Career Guidance

Helping students navigate complex career landscapes, internship opportunities, and professional development paths.

Professional Networking

Creating a strong safety net for alumni to advance their professional careers through referrals and exclusive networking.
